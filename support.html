Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up the integration?
  1. Install the Zoom app from the marketplace
  2. Configure your N8N workflow with the provided template
  3. Set up your Notion database with the required properties
  4. Test the integration with a recorded meeting
Why aren't my meeting transcripts being processed?
Make sure that:
  • Meeting transcription is enabled in your Zoom settings
  • The meeting was recorded
  • Your N8N workflow is active and properly configured
  • API credentials are valid and have proper permissions
Can I customize the task extraction prompts?
Yes! You can modify the AI prompts in the N8N workflow nodes to better suit your meeting structure and task identification needs.
Which AI providers are supported?
Currently, we support OpenAI (GPT-4) and Anthropic (Claude). You can choose your preferred provider in the workflow configuration.
Is my meeting data stored permanently?
No, meeting transcripts are processed in real-time and not permanently stored on our servers. Tasks are created directly in your Notion workspace.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Error 401: Check your API credentials and permissions
  • No tasks generated: Verify the AI analysis step completed successfully
  • Notion creation failed: Ensure your database schema matches the expected format
  • Transcript not found: Confirm meeting transcription was enabled
